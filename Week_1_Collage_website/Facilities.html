Central Library

The Central Library is one of the important facilities of Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology as it caters to the information and research needs of faculty and students. Originated in the year 1998. The Central Library has adopted Open Access System. All the books are arranged classified as per Dewey decimal classification 21st edition which is helpful to the users to locate his / her desired book. Central Library is Fully Automated using LIBSYS10 LMS (Library Management Software). It is Web Centric having support for Twenty Thousand Unique Records with five Concurrent housekeeping clients along with twenty five concurrent users for web OPAC (On – line Public Accessing Catalogue) on server Under Windows 2012 platform. This Software is supporting Barcode system for issue & return of books. The Library is an Institutional member of DELNET. It provides web access to 1,21,08,793 records of Books, Periodicals, Thesis, Dissertations. It is connected with 2196 libraries.

Yoga & Meditation

Yoga and meditation are integral to maintaining mental and physical well-being. The campus offers yoga sessions to help students manage stress, improve concentration, and build self-confidence. Regular practice can enhance overall health and bring about a sense of peace and balance in daily life.

  • Daily Yoga
  • Meditation
  • Yoga Diet Guidance

Importance of Yoga and Meditation

Yoga is a Sanskrit word derived from "yuj," meaning connection, joining, or union of body, mind, and soul. In today’s stressful world, Yoga and Meditation offer a remedy for stress, anxiety, high blood pressure, and related ailments, including heart stroke, brain damage, diabetes, and even lung cancer.

Benefits

  • Maintains healthy functioning of internal organs
  • Helps control chronic conditions like high blood pressure, sugar, and heart problems
  • Improves concentration, memory, willpower, and self-confidence
  • Enhances physical appearance and energy levels

Sports & Games

The Department of Physical Education plays a key role in promoting fitness and recreational activities. The campus has excellent facilities for both indoor and outdoor sports, including basketball, volleyball, badminton, cricket, and a gymnasium for men and women. Students are encouraged to participate in intercollegiate and university tournaments, fostering teamwork and physical wellness.

Outdoor Games

  • Volleyball (4 courts)
  • Basketball (2 courts)
  • Football (practice ground)
  • Ball Badminton (1 court)
  • Throw Ball (1 court)
  • Cricket Nets (1 set)
  • Athletic Field (1 field)

Indoor Games

  • Table Tennis (3 tables)
  • Chess (5 boards)

Fitness Facilities

The gymnasium is equipped for both men and women, providing separate workout spaces. Facilities include horizontal bars, parallel bars, and fitness machines for strength training and cardiovascular health.

Cafeteria

The college cafeteria provides a variety of healthy and delicious food options for students and staff. With a clean and hygienic environment, it serves snacks, meals, and beverages at affordable prices. The spacious seating area makes it a popular spot for students to relax and socialize between classes.

Transport

The college offers a robust transportation system with a fleet of buses that connect the campus to various parts of the city and surrounding areas. The service ensures safe, reliable, and convenient travel for students and staff, reducing commuting challenges and saving time.

Laboratories

PVPSIT is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories that support practical learning across various disciplines. The labs are well-maintained and feature modern equipment, offering students hands-on experience to complement theoretical knowledge. These facilities foster innovation and skill development among students.
